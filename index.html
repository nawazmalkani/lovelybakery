About Lovely Bakery

Founded in 1985 by Husen Bhai Malkani, Lovely Bakery has proudly built a legacy of trust, purity and quality in Palitana.

We strictly follow 100% vegetarian and Jain-friendly practices, maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and freshness every day.


Our bakery is known for freshly prepared Pav, Toast and Khari, crafted daily with consistent taste and quality.

We specialize in premium cakes, chocolates and elegant gift hampers perfect for weddings, Jain functions and festive occasions.


With decades of experience, we proudly serve bulk orders for weddings and events, making every celebration special and memorable.


Every product at Lovely Bakery is prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes that reflect our rich heritage.


Our specialty lies in creating customized gift boxes and hampers that add elegance and value to every celebration.

From daily essentials to festive delights, we maintain same-day freshness and premium quality in every item we serve.


Built on trust and tradition, Lovely Bakery stands as a preferred choice in Palitana.
